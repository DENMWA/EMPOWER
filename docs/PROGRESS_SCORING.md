# Progress Scoring

Default scale:

1. Full support required
2. Substantial support required
3. Moderate support required
4. Minimal support required
5. Independent or consistently demonstrated

Progress classifications:

- Insufficient Evidence
- Baseline Established
- Maintaining
- Emerging Progress
- Progress Observed
- Sustained Progress
- Goal Achieved
- Barrier Observed
- Regression Concern
- Review Required

AI may suggest cautious classifications only. `Goal Achieved`, `Sustained Progress` and `Regression Concern` require authorised confirmation.

Evidence strength considers approved observations, observation period, number of staff, objective detail, manager review, consistency, contradiction, recency and source quality. The score must always be displayed with supporting and limiting factors.
