# AI Safety For Plan Intelligence

AI may:

- Extract plan information.
- Suggest goals and strategies.
- Suggest baseline indicators.
- Suggest goal links.
- Compare notes against baselines.
- Identify evidence gaps.
- Draft cautious progress summaries.

AI must not:

- Verify its own extraction.
- Alter the original plan.
- Invent missing facts.
- Make a diagnosis.
- Determine funding eligibility.
- Determine reportability.
- Claim sustained progress from one note.
- Automatically mark a goal achieved.
- Hide contradictory evidence.

Approved wording:

`The available documentation suggests possible progress. Authorised review is required before updating the verified progress level.`

Approved wording:

`Improvement was observed during this support session. This single observation does not establish sustained progress.`
