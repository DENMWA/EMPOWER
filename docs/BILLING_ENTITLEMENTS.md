# Billing Entitlements

EmpowerNotes supports native scheduling and invoicing across all paid tiers without requiring Xero.

## Solo

- Native invoicing enabled
- Basic scheduling
- Recurring shifts
- Manual pricing upload
- Self-review invoices
- PDF/CSV export
- 100 invoice lines per month
- 10 active service agreements

## Practice

- Team scheduling
- Manager invoice review
- Bulk invoice generation
- Travel, kilometre and non-face-to-face billing flags
- Invoice batches
- Pricing version diff support
- 2,000 invoice lines per month
- 50 active service agreements

## Provider

- Multi-location architecture
- Scheduled invoice generation
- Advanced budget forecasting
- Automatic NDIA pricing import entitlement
- Optional accounting export hooks
- 20,000 invoice lines per month
- 300 active service agreements

## Enterprise

- Contract-based allowances
- API billing access
- Advanced governance
- Optional Xero, MYOB and QuickBooks integration entitlement

Downgrades preserve existing invoices, service agreements and pricing versions. Above-tier creation actions become blocked or read-only rather than deleting data.
